--- order: 4 kind: article title: Lesbian and Gay --- %section %h1 Lesbian and Gay people are homosexual - that is, sexually attracted to people of the same gender. And only people of the same gender. There are other sexualities that deal with people attracted to many genders. %section %h1 Lesbian refers to a woman attracted to women, gay refers to a man attracted to men. Although sometimes people use "gay" as an umbrella term for lesbians and gays. %section %h1 "Androphilia" and "Gynophilia" can also be used. These meaning "Attracted to men, males and masculinity" and the opposite. These labels are common among non-binary people. There's no necessarily codified term for attraction to non-binary people, although "Skoliosexual" has been suggested.
