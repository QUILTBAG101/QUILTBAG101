--- order: 6 kind: article title: Asexuality --- %section %h1 Asexuality is a lack of sexual attraction to anyone. It is not the same as celibacy, the choice not to have sex. Asexuals often refer to themselves as "ace." Aromantic asexuals often use "aro-ace." %section %h1 Asexuals are capable of having sex and masturbating - some do. Some ace people dislike sex, but others can enjoy it. Just because somone has sex, or enjoys it, It doesn't mean they're not asexual. %section %h1 Asexuality is a spectrum. A grey-asexual (Grey-A) can include people who experience sexual attraction or a sex drive very rarely. %section %h1 A Demisexual is a person who doesn't experience sexual attraction without a strong emotional connection. Demisexuals don't feel attraction to people without this bond, rather than making a choice not to have sex with them.
