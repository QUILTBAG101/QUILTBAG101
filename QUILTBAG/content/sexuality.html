--- order: 3 kind: article title: Sexual orientation --- %section %h1 Sexual orientation refers to a person's patterns of sexual attraction. %section %h1 Romantic orientation refers to a person's patterns of desire for romantic relationships with people. %section %h1 Often sexual and romantic orientation are the same but they can be different. You can be pansexual but homoromantic. I.e. you would be sexually attracted to people of all genders but only desiring romantic relationships with someone of the same gender. %section %h1 A lot of the people who have mismatched sexual and romantic orientation are asexual, but it's in no way limited to them.
