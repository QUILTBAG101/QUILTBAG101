--- order: 1 kind: article title: Introduction order: 0 --- %section %h1 QUILTBAG101 is a primer for people who want to know more about alternative sexuality, gender and sex. %section %h1 QUILTBAG+ stands for Queer/Questioning, Unsure, Intersex, Lesbian, Trans (Transgender/Transsexual), Bisexual, Asexual, Gay/Genderqueer and other identities of gender and sexual minorities. %section %h1 The important thing to remember is that QUILTBAG+ people are fundamentally people, regardless of their identity.
