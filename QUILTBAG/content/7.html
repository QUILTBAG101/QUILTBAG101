--- order: 7 kind: article title: Sex and Intersex --- %section %h1 Intersex people do not fit into a binary (male/female) model of sex* Approximately 1% of people fit the label "Intersex"(http://www.isna.org/faq/frequency) Sex isn't binary. Some people's bodies don't fit neatly into the "male" or "female" categories of sex. %section %h1 To force the binary model of sex upon Intersex people they are often sterilized at a young age Preventing this is one of the primary struggles of intersex activists. %section %h1 Sex refers to biological attributes - primary and secondary sexual characteristics, chromosomes and hormones. There are a number of ways you could define sex - a more comprehensive one takes into account a wide range of sex-determined characteristics. %section %h1 Sex is not the same thing as gender.
